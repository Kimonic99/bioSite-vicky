In the ever-evolving landscape of design and aesthetics, I find myself at the thrilling intersection of two seemingly disparate worlds: UX design and professional makeup artistry. It's a fusion that might raise an eyebrow, but it's precisely at this crossroads where I've discovered my unique creative voice. So, welcome to my world, where the principles of design and the artistry of makeup converge to create experiences that are both visually captivating and effortlessly user-centric. Join me on this exhilarating journey as I continue to push the boundaries of creativity, one brushstroke or one pixel at a time.

Entrepreneurial Journey


Much like the meticulous blending of hues on a canvas, my journey as a UX designer involves crafting seamless digital experiences that resonate with users. My fascination with the psychology behind human-computer interaction fuels my drive to design interfaces that not only look visually appealing but also intuitively guide users through their digital journeys. My attention to detail, honed through countless hours perfecting eyeliner flicks and eyeshadow gradients, translates seamlessly into crafting pixel-perfect user interfaces that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also highly functional. Beyond pixels and brushes, my passion lies in understanding the individuality of every face and every user. Whether I'm enhancing natural beauty with makeup or optimizing digital interactions, my philosophy remains the same: it's all about enhancing the user's experience.
